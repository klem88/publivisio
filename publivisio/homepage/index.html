Welcome to PubliVis.io !

PubliVis.io has been designed to help Cyberlibris publishing partners visualize, hence better understand :

  • where and how their books are prescribed,
  • where and what their strengths and weaknesses are,
  • where and how to act to increase visibility and revenues.

PubliVis.io mainly focuses on faculty choices and librarians recommendations.

Four visual market compasses are available. Each compass is dedicated to a specific market.
